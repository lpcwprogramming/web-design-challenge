Latitudes with an Attitude

"What is Winter?" - Someone on the Equator

As we near the equator, temperatures get increasingly hotter. While it should be said that there is diversity in the climates of the equator, generally they're warm year round. This project was created to observe how temperatures change as we get closer to the equator.
